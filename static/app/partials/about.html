What it's for

Accountabillibuddy is a way for you to get into a routine and stick with it. Some people have immense willpower and never procrastinate. Maybe you aren't one of those three people. I built this app to get into an exercise habit. It's really easy to tell yourself "I'll do it tomorrow" when nothing is riding on getting something it done today. It's even easier when you're juggling other responsibilities.

Ok then - what does it do?

One way to stop avoiding something is to avoid doing something worse. Personally, I can't stand random phone calls from robots. I don't like talking on the phone to begin with!

  1. You schedule an item on the home page. Note that you can't schedule anything earlier than tomorrow. You also can't delete or edit a task within 24 hours of it being due.
  2. If you haven't checked in when the time rolls around, the server will call you and yell at you. Otherwise, it leaves you alone. You can only check in within an hour before an event.
  3. If you'd like, you can have Accountabillibuddy tweet you kudos!

Usage/DON'T SKIP THIS!

To prevent mean-spirited pranks, Accountabillibuddy uses two-factor authentication. You'll need to verify your phone number before receiving any calls, and if you sign up for tweets, you'll need to verify your account to Accountabillibuddy via twitter as well.
